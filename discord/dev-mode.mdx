---
title: Developer mode
description: "In this page, we'll show you how to enable and disable the developer mode"
---

### PC Variant

1. go to the gear icon

2. Scroll down till you see "Advanced"

3. Press "Advanced"

4. Press the slide bar of "Developer Mode", If its green (background of it) your done!

### Mobile/Phone Variant

1. Go on your profile picture where it says "You"

2. Go to the Gear icon

3. Scroll down till you see "Advanced"

4. Press "Advanced"

5. Press the slide bar of "Developer mode", if its blue, (background of it), your done!
